Features

Simple Roles

Administrator

Manage the platform users, creation, deletion, access rights, dashboards from a single role.

Publisher

Create your contents, libraries, courses, assign to users, departments, groups.

Learner

Access your courses, complete your learning path, Register for additional courses, view your certifications.

Bi-Lingual

Create your course contents in Bi-Lingual. Based on the users preference the course automatically chooses the language available and displays the courses if the contents are available in that chosen language. Supports Arabic language.

Content Mangment

Re-Use

Re-use your existing documents, presentations, videos, audios that you already have

Social Media

Connect contents from various social Media Platforms directly into the courses (Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, Slideshare, Vimeo, and YouTube)

Assessments

Multiple type of questions and a variety of test types makes learning more involved and fun

Surveys

Surveys can be part of a course or can be independent to collect and analyze the data for any needs

Learning Route

You can define how a course is completed and certifications are issues. They can be by mere completion of the course, marking it manually as completion, or completion certified through an assessment.

Learning Rules

You can define the rules for every course as to pre requirements for taking a content in a course, how many number of attempts on an assessment, what is the passing score etc.

Create your repository

All your assets used in the courses are available in the common repository of your organization which can be reused, including courses, assessments, and surveys.
